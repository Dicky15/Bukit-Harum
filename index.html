Our Premium Products

The finest export quality from the fertile lands of the Indonesian archipelago.

Nutmeg

Nutmeg is one of Indonesia’s premium spices, widely recognized in the international market for its warm aroma and distinctive flavor. Our nutmeg is carefully selected from high-quality harvests, sourced from the best plantations in Indonesia. It undergoes strict drying and sorting processes to maintain its quality, cleanliness, and export standards.

Cloves

Indonesian cloves are high-value spices known for their strong aroma and high essential oil content. We provide carefully selected cloves harvested at the optimal maturity level and processed hygienically to ensure premium quality for food, pharmaceutical, and industrial use.

Mace

Mace is the reddish aril that surrounds the nutmeg seed and is valued for its delicate yet distinctive aroma. Our mace is processed using proper drying techniques to preserve its natural color, fragrance, and essential qualities for the global culinary market.
